Why ride with us?

Language and Belongingness

  • We organize sporting activities like cycling, motorsports, horseriding,surfing and tennis to unite us as one big family.

  • We have foreigners from 76 different countries who are everready to help in interpretation and translation.

  • If you are staying for long, you can find people who speak your local language to help you learn German for free.

  • For exams purposes, we have a special team with great track record who can prepare you for all German Language levels.

Your Stay Guide

  • We don't just make sports. While we excercise or ride, we show new members through town and where they can find what.

  • We make you aware of the laws, regulations and conventions of the German and Berlin Environment.

  • In case you are caught up with laws you were not aware of, we help you with our professional lawyers.
  • For more law knowledge or issues you can book a consultation with our lawyers at the buttom of the page.
